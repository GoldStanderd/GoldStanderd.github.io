The Man Of The Hour

Introducing: Super Uncle

Dear Parent, I know it sounds a bit strange, a man as a child-minder. To be honest, it was a bit strange for me too, at first. After 20 years as an IT specialist, I found myself more and more in demand to look after the children of my two sisters, so they could go out to work. And I found I loved it! And I became their Super Uncle.

  • Education
